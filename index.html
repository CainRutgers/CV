About

Combining a full-time master in medicine with being a PhD candidate and previous national champion in middle-distance runner is a sign of my two best personal characteristics: determination and consistency.

Experience

  • PhD candidate - OLVG hospital (2021-present)

    I work together with medical businesses to research cutting-edge solutions to the problems of today and multiple specialists from the OLVG hospital, UMC-AMC, UMC-VUmc and Deventer Hospital. I specialize in shoulder pathologies, artificial intelligence-based imaging solutions and surgical procedures for shoulder instability and osteoarthritis. Creating a meaningfull impact for patients and making other students enthusiastic for research and guiding them in their processes motivates me.

  • Student tutor - Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam (2021)

    Tutoring first year medicine students to support them in a time of Covid-19 lockdowns.

  • Editor - SlimAcademy (2019-2021)

    Writing additional study material and summarizing existing literature for medicine students.

  • Ambassador - SlimAcademy (2020-2021)

    Organizing and executing communications and working together with student associations.
