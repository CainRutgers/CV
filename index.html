About

Combining a full-time master in medicine with being a PhD candidate and previous national champion in middle-distance runner is a sign of my two best personal characteristics: determination and consistency.

Experience

As a PhD candidate at the OLVG hospital, I am at the forefront of medical innovation, working with medical businesses to find cutting-edge solutions to the problems of today. I collaborate with multiple specialists from top medical institutions such as the OLVG hospital, UMC-AMC, UMC-VUmc and Deventer Hospital. My area of expertise is shoulder pathologies and I have a passion for researching artificial intelligence-based (imaging) solutions and surgical procedures for shoulder disorders. My ultimate goal is to make a meaningful impact on patients and inspire future generations of medical students to become enthusiastic about research.

In addition to my research, I was also a student tutor at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, where I supported first-year medicine students during the challenging time of Covid-19 lockdowns. Additionally, I served as an editor for SlimAcademy, where I wrote study material and summarized existing literature to support medicine students in their education. Also, I was an ambassador for SlimAcademy, where I helped organize and execute communications and worked closely with student associations.
